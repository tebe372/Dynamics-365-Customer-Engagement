# For players and fans in Gamification
First things first—you need to sign in to your [!INCLUDE[pn_gamification_subsequent](includes/pn-gamification-subsequent-md.md)] profile. [!INCLUDE[proc_more_information](includes/proc-more-information-md.md)][Sign in to Gamification](http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/b3513a9c-ff38-4896-a2ad-afdd08d23135)  
  
 Next, add a [!INCLUDE[pn_gamification](includes/pn-gamification-md.md)] profile picture so the other players recognize you. [!INCLUDE[proc_more_information](includes/proc-more-information-md.md)][Personalize your profile](http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/ad24354b-84f8-4127-b44c-09f55a0bd4ce)  
  
 As the game start date approaches, make sure you take a moment to build your roster. [!INCLUDE[proc_more_information](includes/proc-more-information-md.md)][Draft your team](http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/ce3d8906-0c19-4aac-aba6-e9385a21f18d)  
  
 Once the game has started, keep track of how you're doing. [!INCLUDE[proc_more_information](includes/proc-more-information-md.md)][View the leaderboard](http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/088b9a63-9cda-4e45-80b6-5482ac81147b)  
  
 To share your success or congratulate your peers, drop a post on the Smack Talk board. [!INCLUDE[proc_more_information](includes/proc-more-information-md.md)][Post on Smack Talk](http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/958c8d0d-d57e-492b-bb83-1594c4d1a984)  
  
 If you're often on the road to visit customers and prospects, or take a vacation,  make sure you log your time off so other players can adjust their fantasy teams. [!INCLUDE[proc_more_information](includes/proc-more-information-md.md)][Log your time-off](http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/6f66f024-5c8a-49fa-845e-43f66af19799)  
  
 Have a look at the FAQs if you want to know what to do next or have a general question. [!INCLUDE[proc_more_information](includes/proc-more-information-md.md)][Frequently Asked Questions](http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/338b81d8-3dc8-4d0a-bfd0-4ac7a3fb71aa)
