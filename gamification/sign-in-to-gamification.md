<a name="signInWebApp"></a>   
## Sign in to the Gamification web app  
 Once a game is activated, players can  create their profiles and sign in to the [!INCLUDE[pn_gamification_subsequent](../gamification/includes/pn-gamification-subsequent-md.md)] web app directly.  
  
#### Sign in to the web app  
  
1.  Open  the [Gamification sign-in page](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=830344) in your browser.  
  
2.  Enter your user name and password, and then click **Sign in**.
