[!INCLUDE[./descriptions/canbereferencedresponse.md](./descriptions/canbereferencedresponse.md)]



## Properties
The properties of a complex type contain the structured data of the type.
|Name|Type|Nullable|Unicode|Description| 
|----|----|--------|-------|-----------| 
|CanBeReferenced|Edm.Boolean|true|true|Whether the entity can be the primary entity (one) in a one-to-many relationship. |
