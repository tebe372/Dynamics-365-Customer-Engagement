# Disable or remove Dynamics 365 App for Outlook

To disable or remove the App for Outlook add-in follow these steps:

1. From the Outlook **Home** ribbon, select **Get Add-ins**.  

   > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"] 
   > ![Go to the Get Add-ins menu](../media/get-addin-menu.png)  

2. On the Add-ins for Outlook page, select **My add-ins**. 

   > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"] 
   > ![Go to my add-ins](../media/my-addins.png "Go to my add-ins")  

3. Go to the **Dynmics 365** add-in, select ![More actions](../media/more-outlook-button.png) **More actions** > **Remove**. 

   > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"] 
   > ![Select remove to delete the add-in](../media/my-addins.png "remove-addin.png")
