# Add an email recipient as a contact, lead, or account

When you create or receive a new email or appointment where the recipient's email address don't resolve to a record in your Dynamics 365 app.  You have the choice to add the unresolved email address as a Contact, Lead or Account.


In the **Dynamics 365** pane, click (+) and then select **Add as contact** or **Add as lead**.

![Add a contact or lead](media/add-email-contact-lead.png)

> [!NOTE]
> If the sender of the email is unknown to Dynamics 365, when you add the contact or lead, the email is automatically tracked in Dynamics 365 and set regarding this contact or lead. To stop tracking, you can manually untrack the regarding email.
