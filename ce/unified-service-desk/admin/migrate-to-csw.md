# Migrate to Customer Service workspace

Dynamics 365 Customer Service workspace provides a modern customer service experience that optimizes agent productivity and improves customer satisfaction. 

## Why migrate from Unified Service Desk to Customer Service workspace?

By migrating to Customer Service workspace, you get the following benefits.
- Focus on your business processes rather than managing the client desktop
- Modernize your customer service
- Simplify channel integration for enhanced omnichannel experiences
- Personalize your customer experience using AI
- Maintain a single code base
