## Overview

Elevate chat conversation to voice and video call feature helps and the customer you to initiate a voice or video call while you are communicating with a customer via chat. 

As an agent, when you communicate with a customer via chat, there is a huge exchange of information, which might increase the time you spend on resolving the customer issue. Or, the customer prefers to show and explain the issue with a physical productâ€‹. In these scenarios, you or the customer want to initiate a voice or video call while still the chat conversation is active, and resolve the issue quickly. Elevate chat conversation to voice and video call feature helps you and the customer to initiate a voice or vide call.

## Advantages of voice and video call in live chat

- Resolve the customer issue quickly
- Reduces average handle time and improves productivity
- Enhanced customer satisfaction
- Positive relationship with the customers
