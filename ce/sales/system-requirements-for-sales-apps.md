# System requirements for Sales Hub and Sales Professional apps

This article provides information about the prerequisites and system requirements for using the Sales Hub and Sales Professional apps in your organization.  

## Prerequisites

This section lists the prerequisites for using the Sales Hub and Sales Professional apps.

### International availability

Make sure that Dynamics 365 Sales is available in your region. More information: [Dynamics 365 availability and data locations](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dynamics365/get-started/availability).

## System requirements 

The system requirements to use the sales apps are as follows:


|Area  |Requirements  |
|---------|---------|
|License required     | - **Sales Hub app**: Dynamics 365 Sales Premium or Sales Enterprise<br>- **Sales Professional app**: Dynamics 365 Sales Professional        |
|Operating systems and browsers for web and mobile apps     |  [System requirements for Power Apps](/power-apps/limits-and-config)       |
|Network requirements     | If you're planning to use the embedded Teams dialer, see the [ACS network recommendations](/azure/communication-services/concepts/voice-video-calling/network-requirements).         |
|Storage requirements     |   Azure or Teams storage for storing call recordings     |
|Regions supported for product availability  and data location   | [Infrastructure and availability PDF](https://aka.ms/dynamics_365_international_availability_deck)        |
|Languages supported     |   [Infrastructure and availability PDF](https://aka.ms/dynamics_365_international_availability_deck)     |
