# Install ZoomInfo app     
The ZoomInfo app for Dynamics 365 helps the sales teams accelerate business growth with accurate contact and account information in their preferred Dynamics 365 Sales workflows.     
As a Dynamics 365 administrator, you install the ZoomInfo app for administrators to configure export and mapping settings, and sellers to search for and export ZoomInfo data to Dynamics 365 Sales workflows.
