# View and understand Sales Analytics for Dynamics 365 Sales 

The Sales Analytics for Dynamics 365 Sales template application comes with the Sales Analytics for Dynamics 365 Sales report.

The Sales Analytics for Dynamics 365 Sales report has the following pages out of the box.

- Sales Performance
- Sales Leaderboard
- Win/Loss Analysis
- Top Won/Lost Deals
- Sales Pipeline
- Sales Pipeline Leaderboard
- Sales Activity
- Open Activities
- Lead Analysis
- Account Analysis
