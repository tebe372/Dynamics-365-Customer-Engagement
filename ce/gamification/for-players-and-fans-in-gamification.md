# For players and fans in Gamification
First things first—you need to sign in to your [!INCLUDE[pn_gamification_subsequent](includes/pn-gamification-subsequent-md.md)] profile. [!INCLUDE[proc_more_information](includes/proc-more-information-md.md)] [Sign in to Gamification](sign-in-to-gamification.md)  
  
 Next, add a [!INCLUDE[pn_gamification](includes/pn-gamification-md.md)] profile picture so the other players recognize you. [!INCLUDE[proc_more_information](includes/proc-more-information-md.md)] [Set up your profile](set-up-your-profile.md)  
  
 As the game start date approaches, make sure you take a moment to build your roster. [!INCLUDE[proc_more_information](includes/proc-more-information-md.md)] [Manage a fantasy team](manage-a-fantasy-team.md)  
  
 Once the game has started, keep track of how you're doing. [!INCLUDE[proc_more_information](includes/proc-more-information-md.md)] [View the leaderboard](view-the-leaderboard.md)  
  
 To share your success or congratulate your peers, drop a post on the Smack Talk board. [!INCLUDE[proc_more_information](includes/proc-more-information-md.md)] [Post on Smack Talk](post-on-smack-talk.md)  
  
 If you're often on the road to visit customers and prospects, or take a vacation,  make sure you log your time off so other players can adjust their fantasy teams. [!INCLUDE[proc_more_information](includes/proc-more-information-md.md)] [Log your time off](log-your-time-off.md)  
  
 Have a look at the FAQs if you want to know what to do next or have a general question. [!INCLUDE[proc_more_information](includes/proc-more-information-md.md)] [Frequently asked questions about Dynamics 365 - Gamification](frequently-asked-questions-about-dynamics-365-gamification.md)
