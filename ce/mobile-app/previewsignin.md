# Preview: Introducing new sign in experience for iOS users 

[!INCLUDE [cc-beta-prerelease-disclaimer](../includes/cc-beta-prerelease-disclaimer.md)]

> [!IMPORTANT]
> - This feature currently has limited availability.
> - Email address sign in option is available for online users only.

Sign in to the Dynamics 365 mobile app quickly using your work email address. 

## Sign in using your email address

To sign in, using your work email address, enter your email address, and then select ![Sign in](media/signinarrow.png "Dynamics 365  for phones and tablets Preview sign-in").

![Enter email address](media/previewsignin.png "Enter email address to sign-in in").


### Basic navigation

1.	When you sign in, you will see a list of apps that you have acceess to.

> [!NOTE]
> If you have access to multiple organizations then you will see all the apps that you have access to across organizations.

2.	Select the app that you want to use.

![Select an app to use](media/openanapp.png "Select an app to use"). 

### Sign out

From the top right, select ![Sign out](media/gearsignout.png "Sign out") > **Sign out**.

![Sign out](media/signout1.png "Sign out")

## Sign in using web address

You can also sign in using your Dynamics 365 Customer Engagement app web address and user name and password. 

1. On the sign-in screen screen choose **Sign in with a web address instead** option.

![Enter user name and password address](media/userandpassword.png "Enter your user name and password"). 

> [!NOTE]
> If you need help with your sign in information, contact your Dynamics 365 admin

2. Enter the URL for your Dynamics 365 Customer Engagement app and then select ![Sign in](media/signinarrow.png "Dynamics 365  for phones and tablets Preview sign-in") and enter your user name and password.
