# Billing and subscription support
<a name="supportTopic"></a> ![Microsoft Dynamics 365 product family](../admin/media/product-family.png "Dynamics 365 product family")  
  
<a name="topSection1"></a>  
  
## Dynamics 365 (online)  
 For billing support for [!INCLUDE[pn_CRM_online](../includes/pn-crm-online.md)]:  
  
-   Find your local [!INCLUDE[pn_CRM_online](../includes/pn-crm-online.md)] [billing support number](http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?LinkID=278814).  
  
-   Or, [sign in to your work or school account](http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?LinkId=396466) and submit a web ticket.  

## Dynamics 365 on-premises  
 Your support options depend on how you purchased [!INCLUDE[pn_microsoftcrm](../includes/pn-microsoftcrm.md)] (on-premises):  
  
-   If you purchased through volume licensing, see [Volume licensing](http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?LinkId=396468).  
  
-   If you purchased through [!INCLUDE[pn_Microsoft_Dynamics](../includes/pn-microsoft-dynamics.md)], see [Microsoft Dynamics](http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?LinkId=396467).  
  
## Social Engagement  
 For billing support for [!INCLUDE[pn_netbreeze_long](../includes/pn-netbreeze-long.md)]:  
  
-   Find your local [!INCLUDE[pn_CRM_online](../includes/pn-crm-online.md)] [billing support number](http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?LinkID=278814).  
  
-   Or, [sign in to your work or school account](http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?LinkId=396466) and submit a web ticket.  
  
## Product support  
 If you have questions on how to use or technical issues using [!INCLUDE[pn_microsoftcrm](../includes/pn-microsoftcrm.md)], see: [Product Support](../admin/product-support.md).
