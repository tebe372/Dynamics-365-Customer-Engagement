# Deliver web engagement experience with portal capabilities for Microsoft Dynamics 365

Portal capabilities for Microsoft Dynamics CRM brings qualified expertise in web engagement and CMS frameworks with an integrated bundle of web portal solutions that add sophisticated content management, knowledge and case management, opportunity management, membership, profile, and self-service capabilities to Dynamics CRM. Use portals to set up an interactive, web-based sales, services, support and social engagement application platform to deliver powerful, easy-to-build, self-service solutions that helps you to
- Grow communities and deepen relationship with your users. More information: Engage with communities using the community portal. 
- Use the front-side editing engine to publish content on a portal, plus a guide to the entities that make up the portals content management system. More information: Manage portal content. 
- Enable partner opportunity registration, management and routing. Your partners can manage these opportunities including updating their progress on opportunities that have been accepted. More information: Empower your channel partners. 
- Configure the portals and customize Dynamics CRM to modify the behavior or visual style of the portal, add dynamic content to the pages, assign rights and permissions to secure content and much more. More information: Configure a CRM portal.
