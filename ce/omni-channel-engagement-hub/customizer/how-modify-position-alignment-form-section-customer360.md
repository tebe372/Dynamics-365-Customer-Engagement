# How to modify the position or alignment of the form (card) in the Customer 360

1.  Sign in to Dynamics 365.

2.  Go to **Settings** &gt; **Customization** &gt; **Customize the system**.

3.  Expand **Components** &gt; **Entities** &gt; **Live Work Item** and select **Forms**.

4.  Select **Session Overview Form** from the list.

 ![modify cusomter 360 form section](../media/oc-crm-modify-customer360form.png "Modify cusomter 360 form section")  

5.  Drag and drop the form (card) in the order you want to align.

6.  Select **Save** and select **Publish**.
