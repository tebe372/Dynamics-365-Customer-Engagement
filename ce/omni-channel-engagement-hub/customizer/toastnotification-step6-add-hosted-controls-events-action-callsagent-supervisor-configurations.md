# Step 6: Add the hosted controls, events, and action calls to the agent and supervisor configurations 

1.  Sign in to Dynamics 365. 

<!-- -->

1.  Go to **Settings** &gt; **Unified Service Desk** &gt; **Configuration**. 

<!-- -->

1.  Select the configuration from the list. 

<!-- -->

1.  On the nav bar, click the down arrow next to Configuration, and select Action Calls. 

<!-- -->

1.  On the next page, click Add Existing Action Call, type the name of the action in the search bar, and then press ENTER or click the search icon. 

<!-- -->

1.  Add the Action calls that are displayed in the search results. 

<!-- -->

1.  Similarly, add the hosted controls and the events by clicking the down arrow next to Configuration, and clicking Hosted Controls and Events, respectively. 

<!-- -->

1.  Click Save.
